Work Process (Step-by-Step)

1. Discussion

We start with a detailed conversation about your business, goals, target audience, and design preferences. I gather all requirements to fully understand your vision.

2. Research & Planning

I analyze competitors, choose the right tech stack, and create a clear structure (wireframe) for your website to ensure best performance and user experience.

3. UI/UX Design

I design a modern, premium-looking interface with clean layout, colors, and typography focused on conversion and user engagement.

4. Development

Your website is built using clean, optimized code. It will be fully responsive, fast-loading, and compatible across all devices.

5. Testing & Optimization

I test your website on multiple devices and browsers. Speed, SEO basics, and performance are optimized before delivery.

6. Delivery & Revisions

I deliver the final website and make any required revisions to ensure you are 100% satisfied with the result.

7. Launch

I help you deploy your website on hosting (GitHub, Netlify, or custom server) and make it live for the world.

8. Support & Maintenance

Even after delivery, I provide support for updates, bug fixes, and improvements to help your business grow.
